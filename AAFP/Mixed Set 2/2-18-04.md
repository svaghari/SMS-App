Perform spirometry

The American Academy of Asthma, Allergy, and Immunology recommends that asthma not be diagnosed or treated without spirometry. Once the diagnosis is confirmed, treatment should commence with a short-acting Î²-agonist as needed, followed by stepwise treatment based on the severity of asthma.
